Acharya on the way

Acharya on the way is not a teaching — it's a walk. A space for questions that don't have neat answers: about Bharatha, about culture, about the older ideas we've stopped asking about. It's less about having arrived at wisdom, and more about staying honest in the search for it. If you've ever paused mid-scroll and wondered something you couldn't quite name — this is for you.
